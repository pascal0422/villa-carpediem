The Philosophy

Not a Rental. A Private Resort Experience.

Villa Carpe Diem was conceived for those who understand that true luxury is not measured in stars — but in stillness, space, and the unhurried passage of a Provençal afternoon. Every surface, every sightline, every moment has been considered.

This is not a house you book. It is an estate you inhabit — with the service, privacy, and artistry of the world's finest resort experiences, yet with the warmth and intimacy that only a private home can offer.

"We created a place where our guests feel they have discovered something rare — because they have."

The Master Suite

A Sanctuary Within a Sanctuary

The ground-floor master suite opens directly onto the pool terrace through floor-to-ceiling glass — a seamless threshold between private retreat and Mediterranean sun. Sculptural furnishings by leading European ateliers create an atmosphere of serene indulgence.

An organic-form vanity alcove and bespoke lighting design ensure every detail speaks to quiet, uncompromising refinement.

The Grand Salon

Designed for the Art of Gathering

Organic curves, natural oak floors, and a signature Vertigo chandelier define this light-filled living space. The expansive glass wall retracts fully, dissolving the boundary between indoors and the sun-drenched terrace beyond.

Whether hosting an intimate dinner or simply unwinding with a glass of Bandol rosé, this is where the spirit of Carpe Diem comes to life.

The Dining Pavilion

Under the Provençal Sky

A cathedral-beamed open-air pavilion frames every meal as an occasion. The grand table seats twelve beneath hand-woven pendant lighting, overlooking the pool and ancient olive trees beyond.

Arrange a private chef for the evening, and let the golden hour do the rest. This is outdoor dining elevated to an art form.

The Rooftop Terrace

Above the Treetops, Beneath the Stars

Ascend to a private rooftop solarium where panoramic views stretch across the Provençal canopy. Designer daybeds and an intimate lounge create the perfect perch for morning coffee or a late-evening digestif under the southern sky.

Each of the six bedroom suites has been individually designed with its own character — from sculptural headboards to bespoke vanities — yet all share the same uncompromising commitment to comfort and beauty.

The Kitchen

Equipped by Gaggenau

A chef's dream realised in natural stone and Vicenza oak. The full Gaggenau Série 400 installation — from the Vario Domino gas cooktop to the combi steam oven, warming drawer, and dual Froid Vario columns — ensures every culinary ambition is met with precision.

Complemented by an EuroCave wine cellar, a Hoshizaki professional ice maker, and Blanco stainless steel workstations, this kitchen stands ready for Michelin-level private dining.

Cave à Vin & Cellier

Where Every Bottle Finds Its Place

Designed by Tomassi Galanti in Vicenza oak and matte lacquer, the dedicated wine cellar and pantry elevate private hospitality to its finest expression.

Private Collection

Cave à Vin & Cellier

The temperature-controlled cave à vin, fitted with bespoke Vicenza oak cabinetry and LED 3000K ambient lighting, holds a curated collection behind individually hinged oak-panel doors. The EuroCave S-INSP-S ensures optimal conditions for every vintage.

Adjacent, the cellier serves as both pantry and service area — housing the Hoshizaki ice maker, additional cold storage, and the Gaggenau dishwasher — all concealed behind seamless panels matching the kitchen's material language.

The Location

Chemin des Salins, Saint-Tropez

Set along the coveted Chemin des Salins — one of Saint-Tropez's most prestigious private addresses — Villa Carpe Diem offers the rare balance of absolute privacy and effortless proximity. The legendary port, Pampelonne's beaches, and the village's finest establishments are all moments away.

Yet within the estate's walls, the world recedes entirely. Only birdsong, the rustle of olive branches, and the gentle lap of your private pool remain.
